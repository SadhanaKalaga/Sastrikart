Our Story

Connecting devotees with authentic Vedic priests

SastriKart was born from a simple idea - to make traditional Vedic rituals accessible to everyone in the modern world. Founded by a group of Sanskrit scholars and tech enthusiasts, we bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and contemporary convenience.

We began as a small service helping families across India, to help them perform rituals with authenticity and ease.

Proudly incubated under the guidance of VISHVA TBI.

Our Mission

To preserve and propagate authentic Vedic traditions by connecting qualified priests with devotees, ensuring rituals are performed with proper procedures, mantras, and devotion.

Our Promise

We rigorously verify every priest's qualifications, ensure all rituals follow proper Vedic procedures, and provide complete transparency in pricing and services.
